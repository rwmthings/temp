About Us

Kendo focuses on practicing the 'Way of the Sword' of ancient Japan. The practice of Kendo comes with the goal of self-discipline through the use of a shinai (sword). It is also a competitive sport, where schools come together to compete and help recognize ones shortcomings and help them grow as a practitioner.

All currently enrolled students and/or staff members at UWM are elgible to participate. There is no experience necessary to join the Cornell kendo club, and everyone - regardless of sex, age, or physical condition - is encouraged to apply and join! Feel free to come watch or join practices to get an idea of what kendo is about and if it's a good option for you.
